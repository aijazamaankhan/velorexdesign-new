      Our Story

      Welcome to Velorex Design, the creative wing of the Velorex family. Born out of a passion for intricate craftsmanship and bold aesthetics, we specialize in high-quality handcrafted wood items, mesmerizing resin arts, and professional graphic design services.

      Every physical piece in our store is painstakingly handcrafted by skilled artisans who pour their heart and soul into the work. Our digital design team brings the same level of meticulous attention to detail to elevate your brand's identity.

      Our Mission

      To blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design principles, delivering products and services that inspire creativity and bring joy to our customers' lives.

      The Velorex Family

      We are proud to be part of a larger family of premium brands, including Velorex Music and Velorex Garments. Excellence is in our DNA.

      2. Handcrafted Variations

      Our wood items and resin arts are 100% handcrafted. As a result, slight variations in color, texture, wood grain, and resin flow are natural and expected. These are not defects but rather proof of the item's uniqueness.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Everything you need to know about Velorex Design

      How do I care for my wood products?
      Hand wash with mild soap and water. Do not soak. Periodically apply food-safe mineral oil or beeswax to maintain the finish and prevent cracking.
      Is the resin food safe?
      Yes, we use high-quality, non-toxic, and FDA-compliant epoxy resin for all our serving boards and trays. However, we recommend avoiding direct cutting on the resin surface.
      Do you take custom commissions?
      Absolutely! We love creating bespoke pieces. Whether it's a custom-sized table, a specific resin color palette, or a unique brand identity, reach out to us via WhatsApp.
      How long does shipping take?
      Standard items are shipped within 2-3 business days. Custom handcrafted pieces may take 7-14 business days to prepare before shipping.

      Our Policy

      Due to the handcrafted and customized nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges unless the product is delivered in a damaged condition.

      If you receive a damaged product, please take a photo/video and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or a full refund after verifying the damage.
